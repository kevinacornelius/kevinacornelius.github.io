Latest Album

Written and released between June and October 2018, piano music is a collection of original piano pieces. The album is my first foray into writing music for piano.

  • Songs is a collection of five unrelated musical works in which I gently experiment with harmony and style.
  • Echo takes a melody and harmonizes it with the same melody an octave lower and displaced by three beats.
  • Late Afternoon Thoughts in the Sun begins a transition in the album from academic exploration to creating moods. Late Afternoon Thoughts in the Sun captures the essence of being in deep thought in the late afternoon sun.
  • Choosing Something Else is a whimsical response to people suggesting that I follow a stable, non-music career: "Thanks for your suggestions. I am going to do things my own way and choose something else, a path of my own choosing."
  • Window View exudes the joy that I experienced each time I looked out of the windows of my former Jersey City, NJ apartment.
  • Derived from an English Horn theme in an orchestral movement I had once drafted, Finding Center brings the album to a pensive, self-reflective conclusion.
